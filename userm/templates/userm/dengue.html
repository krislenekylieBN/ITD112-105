Dengue Dataset

Through the bite of an infected Aedes species mosquito (Ae. aegypti or Ae. albopictus), dengue viruses are transmitted to people. 4 billion people, or close to half of the world's population, reside in dengue-risk zones. In unsafe locations, dengue is frequently the main cause of sickness.

Confirmed dengue cases and fatalities in the Philippines, according to the Department of Health's Epidemiology Bureau.
